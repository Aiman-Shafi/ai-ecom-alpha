FAQ

Got questions?
We've got answers. 👇

Where does the ad data come from?
Genie OS is powered by the Foreplay API, which indexes over 100 million ads across Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, and Google. Data is refreshed continuously so you always see current, live campaigns.
How does the "Proven Winner" badge work?
Badges are fully data-driven based on how long an ad has been running. Brands don't waste money on ads that don't convert, so longevity is the best proxy for performance. Proven Winner = 30+ days, Strong Signal = 14–30 days, Early Traction = 7–14 days.
Can I track specific competitors?
Yes — search any brand in the Knowledge Base, add them as a competitor, and their full ad feed appears on your dashboard sorted by longevity. Track unlimited brands, filter by platform, niche, or minimum running days.
How does the AI generation work?
Set up your brand context once — voice, audience, USPs, colours — in the Knowledge Base. When you generate from a winning ad, the AI uses both the reference ad's strategy and your brand context to produce on-brand copy, angles, and hooks ready to brief your creative team.
Is there a free plan?
Yes — you can sign up and start discovering ads, track up to 2 competitors, and run a limited number of AI analyses completely free. No credit card required.
